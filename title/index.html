About Me

Biography

I am Leiqi Ye, a second-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, advised by Dr. Elizabeth Polgreen and Dr. Jianyi Cheng. Prior to my PhD, I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Prof. Yatin Manerkar.

My research interests lie in Hardware Formal Verification and Program Synthesis, with a broader goal of improving the reliability and automation of hardware design, as well as enhancing the scalability of verification techniques.
